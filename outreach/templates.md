# Outreach Templates

## First message

Hello [Name], I noticed [specific website issue] while looking for dental clinics in [area]. I prepared a small unofficial concept showing how patients could find your services and contact options more easily on mobile. May I send the preview link?

## Follow-up

Hello [Name], following up on the website concept I mentioned. It focuses on one practical improvement: [highest-value improvement]. No obligation; I would appreciate your feedback even if the timing is not right.

## Rules

- Mention one observed issue, not generic praise.
- Ask permission before sending a personalised live concept.
- Never imply affiliation with the business.
- Do not make medical or performance claims.
- Stop after one follow-up unless the prospect replies.
